App Name Concepts

Side Games Tracker for Disc Golf, Cornhole, Horseshoes & More

Why "Birdies and Bourbon" Needs to Change

  • Too niche: Not everyone drinks bourbon, alienates non-drinkers
  • Activity-specific: "Birdies" locks us to disc golf only
  • Limited expansion: Can't add cornhole, horseshoes, or other yard games
  • Not memorable: Doesn't capture the competitive, side-bet vibe
SideShow
"The side games that steal the spotlight."
Frames bets and modifiers as the MAIN entertainment. Turns any casual outdoor game into a spectacle. Works across all activities - disc golf, cornhole, horseshoes, anything.

Why This Works

  • Activity-agnostic (works for everything)
  • Memorable, single word, easy to say
  • Available domain: sideshow.app
  • Theater/entertainment vibe = fun, social
  • Logo works at all sizes (ticket + QR)
⭐ Top Recommendation
YardShark
"For the undisputed champion of the lawn."
Playful nod to "card shark" or "pool shark." Implies competitive skill and friendly wagering. The shark fin cutting through grass is instantly recognizable.

Why This Works

  • Fun, aggressive, competitive vibe
  • Works for all yard games
  • Great merch potential (shark fin icon)
  • Easy to remember and spell
AnteUp
"Don't just play—make it interesting."
Action-oriented betting phrase. Challenges players to put skin in the game. Stack of chips with upward arrow = raise the stakes.

Why This Works

  • Classic betting terminology
  • Two-word name is memorable
  • Clear call-to-action vibe
  • Works across all betting/skins games
Wildcard
"Add a little chaos to your game day."
Highlights card-based modifiers and custom rules. Playing card with outdoor sun = indoors/outdoors fusion. Unpredictable and fun.

Why This Works

  • One word, easy to remember
  • Card game association is perfect
  • Outdoor + card visual works great
  • Implies flexibility and customization
TrashTalk
"Play the cards you're dealt, heckle the rest."
Captures competitive banter and disruptive card modifiers. Speech bubble + wild card = talking smack with game-changing plays.

Why This Works

  • Perfect for competitive friend groups
  • Cheeky, memorable name
  • Social aspect built into brand
  • Logo tells the whole story
Showdown
"Where casual yard games get serious."
Dramatic, face-to-face challenge. Two lines colliding = head-to-head competition. Perfect for final skins matches and tiebreakers.

Why This Works

  • Strong, action-oriented name
  • Western duel vibe = serious stakes
  • One word, easy domain
  • Logo is clean and modern
