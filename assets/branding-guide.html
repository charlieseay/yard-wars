Design Principles

OLED Optimized

True black (#000000) background turns off pixels on OLED screens, maximizing battery life during outdoor rounds.

High Contrast

Neon colors on black provide maximum visibility in bright sunlight. No pastels, no gradients that fade in daylight.

Fat-Finger Friendly

Minimum 48dp tap targets for outdoor use. Glove-friendly spacing. Score buttons sized for quick taps.

Gesture-First

Drag-drop chips, swipe actions, long-press details. Minimize modal dialogs and keyboard input.

Smart Defaults

Pre-fill scores to par. Tap only deviations. Push skins automatically. Reduce cognitive load mid-round.

Offline-First

Zero network calls. 100% local storage. No loading spinners. Crash recovery with atomic writes.
