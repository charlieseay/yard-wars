Design System

  • Background: True black (#000000) - OLED pixels off = battery savings
  • Disc / Skins: Neon green (#00FF00) - high visibility outdoors
  • Bourbon: Amber (#FF8C00) - warm, appetizing
  • Ice / Accent: Cyan (#00FFFF) - cool contrast
  • Text: Pure white (#FFFFFF) - maximum contrast

Icon Philosophy

  • Dual Identity: Bourbon glass (top) + disc golf disc (bottom) = name's two halves
  • OLED Optimized: True black background turns off pixels on OLED screens
  • High Contrast: Neon colors pop in sunlight (outdoor app)
  • Scalable: Simple shapes work at all sizes (1024px → 29px)
  • Memorable: Unique combo no other disc golf app has
