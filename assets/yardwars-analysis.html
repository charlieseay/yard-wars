YARDWARS

Friendly games. Serious stakes.
Overall Grade: B+

The Verdict

Why It Works

  • High energy and memorable — Easy to pronounce, spell, and recall with strong phonetic impact
  • Thematically aligned — "Wars" fits card modifiers (sabotage, shields, attacks) much better than passive scoring
  • Socially competitive — Immediately frames app around friendly rivalry, betting, and bragging rights
  • Anchored to outdoors — "Yard" cues casual outdoor play (backyards, courses, parks) without sounding corporate

Potential Issues

  • Scale limitation — "Yard" might feel too small/casual for premium disc golf courses
  • Domain saturation — Was a DIY Network TV show, common in landscaping/mobile gaming
  • Tone sensitivity — "Wars" could feel juvenile or aggressive to minimalist users
