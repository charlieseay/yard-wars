⚡ Key Features

🎯
Offline-First
Zero network required. All data stored locally with atomic writes. Crash recovery built-in.
🔋
OLED Optimized
True black background (#000000) turns pixels off on OLED screens. Maximum battery life for all-day rounds.
🎨
Custom Games
Full workshop to create custom decks with your own chips and cards. Save and reuse across rounds.
📊
Smart Payouts
Individual Ledger Method calculates exact peer-to-peer payouts. Venmo integration for instant settling.
🥃
Bourbon Passport
Track career stats, distillery check-ins, and multi-course expeditions. Regional packs with geofencing.
📱
QR Sync
Share scorecards peer-to-peer via QR code. Minified payload under 1KB for fast outdoor scanning.
